Your Public Information

Cascade Studio is a public platform. As such, all the projects and content you share as "Public" become visible to everybody. In particular,
  • Content shared as Public is accessible and searchable in the Cascade Studio Browse window, but can also be accessible to third party applications or services that use Cascade Studio's public firebase API.
  • Content shared as Private is visible to anyone who has an embed/iframe or direct link to your published content, but won't show up in the Browse section of the web.

Cascade Studio is also a public community. Your profile, including your username, avatar picture, and "about yourself" sections are visible to anyone who visits Cascade Studio. You can edit your profile information and picture at any time.

Similarly, your interactions and activity on Cascade Studio such as your comments and likes is public as well. You can delete comments, edit likes, delete projects and playlists, picture and edit your profile information and picture at any time.

Cookies and Analytics

Cookies are required to use Cascade Studio, they are used to help the system identify users and maintain their session open while they work in the website. Without cookies Cascade Studio won't be able to store progress outside of the url, and it won't be able to connecting to the database.

Cascade Studio does not use cookies to track any of the users actions in the site, or store any of the content they type of search for.

Cascade Studio collects global, non user-specific information regarding overal website traffic volume, frequency of visits, type of browser and operating system. Cascade Studio employes Google Analytics.
